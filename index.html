Welcome to Splashes!

Welcome to Splashes! Step into a world where the crystal-clear waters of the pool beckon, inviting you to experience the ultimate refreshment and relaxation. Whether you're a dedicated lap swimmer, a sun-soaked lounging enthusiast, or a spirited cannonball connoisseur, our aquatic oasis is your haven. Here, the gentle lull of water and the joyous laughter of poolside fun come together to create moments of pure bliss. Dive into the serene depths, bask in the warm embrace of the sun, and let the cool waters of our pool wash away your cares. At Splashes, we celebrate the magic of swimming in the pool, where every plunge is an opportunity to make a memory.

Family Swim

Parent & Child Family Time

We believe in the power of family and the pure joy of sharing moments together. Our family swim experience is a celebration of togetherness, where parents, children, and all loved ones can unite in the embrace of our inviting waters. Whether it's teaching your little ones to float, enjoying friendly races, or simply relishing the laughter that fills the air, our family swim sessions create lasting bonds and cherished memories. At Splashes, we understand the importance of these shared moments, and we're here to make every family swim an unforgettable and heartwarming experience. Dive in with your loved ones, create beautiful memories, and let Splashes be the backdrop for your family's aquatic adventures.

Infant Swim

Baby Swimming in the Safety Area

We understand that the safety and well-being of your littlest family members are of utmost importance. Our Infant Swim program is dedicated to providing a secure and nurturing environment for babies as they take their first steps into the aquatic world. Our certified instructors are trained to guide infants through gentle water introduction, helping them develop essential water-safety skills. We prioritize the comfort and confidence of both babies and parents, ensuring that every experience is a positive and joyful one. At Splashes, we're committed to making infant swim a valuable and bonding experience for you and your little one, setting the foundation for a lifetime of water enjoyment.

Water Safety

Daily Check Under Protection

Your safety is our top priority, and we are dedicated to providing a secure and worry-free environment. Our daily check under protection ensures that all safety measures are rigorously maintained to guarantee your peace of mind. From assessing water quality and temperature to inspecting all safety equipment and lifeguard readiness, we leave no stone unturned in our commitment to creating a secure space for you and your loved ones. At Splashes, you can relax and enjoy your time in the water, knowing that our vigilance in water safety is a daily promise that we uphold with utmost care and diligence.

So what's this all about?

Our swimming pool isn't just a body of water; it's a space for learning, recreation, and well-being. Our expert instructors are here to guide you through the intricacies of swimming, from stroke techniques to water safety. Whether you're a beginner looking to conquer your fear of the water or an advanced swimmer striving for perfection, our pool is your canvas. We offer a range of classes tailored to various skill levels and interests, ensuring that everyone can find their niche in our aquatic paradise. Join us at Splashes, where our pool is not just a place to swim, but a platform for your aquatic journey and a source of endless inspiration.
